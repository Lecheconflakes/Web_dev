||What to know about F1||

What Even Is Formula 1?

F1, or Formula 1, is the highest class of international single-seater auto racing, considered the pinnacle of motorsport. It is a global team sport where drivers compete for the World Championship while their teams fight for the Constructors' Championship. The "formula" in the name refers to the set of rules all participants must follow regarding their cars.

Facts About Formula 1

Formula 1 is a high-tech racing sport featuring the world's fastest regulated cars, which use advanced aerodynamics to achieve extreme speeds and cornering forces. Key facts include the immense physical demands on drivers, the massive team sizes, the incredible cost of running a team, and the highly specialized and expensive technology of the cars, like their short-lived engines.
