Toyota Supra MK4

The Toyota Supra MK4 is a legendary JDM car known for its powerful engine and sleek design. It was produced from 1993 to 2002 and has become a favorite among car enthusiasts and tuners. The Supra gained even more popularity after being featured in the Fast and Furious movie.
